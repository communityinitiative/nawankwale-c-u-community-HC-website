About Us

Nawankwale C/U Community Health Centre is a non-profit medical facility providing quality, affordable, and compassionate care to residents of Nabitende Subcounty and beyond. Our mission is to serve the health needs of our community with integrity and dedication.

Our Services

  • Outpatient Services
  • Diagnostic Services
  • Maternal Services
  • Youth Friendly Services
  • Inpatient Services
  • Minor Operations
  • Dental Services
  • Preventive Services
  • Rehabilitative Services
  • Cancer Screening Services
  • Epidemiological Surveillance Services
  • Prevention and Management of NTDs/HIV/TB Services

Community Outreach

We extend healthcare beyond our walls through health camps, immunization drives, and awareness programs targeting vulnerable populations.
